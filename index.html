Cat Climb

Guide the cat safely through a dreamy sky climb.

Tutorial

  • Goal: Climb from Earth to Space before you run out of lives.
  • Move: Left / Right arrows or A / D.
  • Jump: Up arrow, W, or Space (double jump is enabled).
  • Crouch/Crawl: Hold Down arrow or S to crouch and crawl.
  • Mobile: Use on-screen Left/Right/Down/Up buttons.
  • Ice Platforms: Ice appears high up and is slippery, so time jumps carefully.
  • Coins + Shop: Collect coins and spend them on hats from the Shop button.
  • Safety: Reset and Quit will show a warning before action.
