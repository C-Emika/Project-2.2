Cat Climb

Guide the cat safely through a dreamy sky climb.

Tutorial

  • Your goal is to reach space, do so by climbing upward.
  • Ensure your lives last to reach your goal.
  • Collect coins from platforms during your climb.
  • Track your progress in meters. Your goal is at 1000 meters.
  • Click the hats button to buy and equip hats with coins. Some hats will need to be obtained through achievements.
  • Click the achievements button to view unlock goals and track your progress.
  • Click the reset button to reset your progress and start from the beginning.
  • Click the quit button to save your current progress and return to the title.
