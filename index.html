Cat Climb

Guide the cat safely through a dreamy sky climb.

How to Play

  • Use Left / Right arrow keys or A / D to move.
  • Press Up arrow or W / Space to jump.
  • The cat can double jump to reach higher platforms.
  • Use 9 lives to climb from the ground to the top of space.
